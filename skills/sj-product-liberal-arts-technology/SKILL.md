# Liberal arts and technology

## Core Move

Connect engineering with taste, story, culture, and emotion.

## Workflow

1. Name the technical capability and the human situation it enters.
2. Identify cultural, emotional, historical, ethical, or expressive meaning the product currently ignores.
3. Inspect language, interaction, form, and story for machine-centered assumptions.
4. Add one humanities-informed choice that changes use rather than merely decorating it.
5. Define a prototype that tests whether the product feels more meaningful and legible.

- Respect the user's context rather than imposing the maker's taste.
- Do not use long quotations from the source material.
