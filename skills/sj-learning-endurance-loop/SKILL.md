# Endurance loop

## Core Move

Design the repeated effort needed for mastery.

## Workflow

1. Define the capability and the smallest representative rep that exercises it.
2. Set a sustainable cadence, quality threshold, feedback source, and recovery allowance.
3. Track completed reps and one diagnostic signal without turning measurement into the work.
4. At fixed intervals, increase difficulty, correct technique, or change the environment based on evidence.
5. Stop, rest, or redesign when pain, declining quality, or life costs breach the agreed boundary.

## Output

Return a compact result with:

- Capability and rep
- Cadence and quality bar
- Feedback signal
- Progression rule
- Recovery and stop boundary

## Guardrails

- Repetition without feedback can automate the wrong behavior.
- Endurance is not permission to ignore injury or burnout.
- Do not count trivial repetitions that avoid the real skill.
