# Fragile Idea Protection

## Core Move

Create a protected space where a rough but promising idea can become concrete enough to deserve judgment.

## Workflow

1. Name the fragile idea and why it might matter.
2. Identify the premature forces that could flatten it.
3. Define the minimum protection window and artifact standard.
4. Decide who should inspect it and who should wait.
5. Set the moment when the idea must face honest review.

- Threats to the idea
- Protection plan
- Inspection circle
- Review trigger

## Guardrails

- Protection is temporary, not a shield from reality.
- Do not protect ideas with no user or product promise.
- Invite critique once the idea has enough form to be judged fairly.
- Do not use long quotations from the source material.
