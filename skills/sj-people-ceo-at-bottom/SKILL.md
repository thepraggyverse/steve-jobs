# CEO at bottom

## Core Move

Treat leadership as service to the builders.

## Workflow

1. Map who creates user value, who enables that work, and where authority currently sits.
2. Identify decisions or reporting burdens that serve executive convenience over frontline effectiveness.
3. Move context, resources, and decision rights toward the people closest to the work while preserving accountability.
4. Define the executive's service obligations and the few decisions that remain centralized.
5. Measure whether cycle time, quality, and frontline judgment improve after the inversion.

## Guardrails

- Inversion is not abdication of strategy, ethics, or accountability.
- Do not push decisions downward without context and resources.
- Avoid turning servant leadership into performative humility.
