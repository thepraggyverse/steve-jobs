# Ambush distribution

## Core Move

Meet customers where attention and traffic already are.

## Workflow

1. Map where target customers already gather, decide, learn, and buy, including channels competitors overlook.
2. Score each channel by audience concentration, access cost, trust, timing, and incumbent defensibility.
3. Find a lawful asymmetric entry point where a small presence can create discovery or switching momentum.
4. Design a bounded channel experiment with an offer, owner, budget, conversion path, and stop condition.
5. Compare acquired learning and durable access against channel dependency before scaling.

## Guardrails

- Do not confuse surprise with deception or unauthorized access.
- Account for platform rules, partner incentives, and channel concentration risk.
- A clever launch is not a distribution system until repeatability is proven.
