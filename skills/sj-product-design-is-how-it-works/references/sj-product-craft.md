# SJ Product Craft

Core themes: make something wonderful, start from the customer experience, bridge technology and liberal arts, simplify to one clear path, make the product explain itself, treat design as how it works, and care about hidden quality.

Use for product, UI, artifact, demo, taste, speed, and end-to-end experience reviews. Favor visible artifacts over abstract claims. Look for the one thing the product should do, the one feeling it should create, and the next concrete iteration.
