# Permanent ensemble

## Core Move

Build a recurring creative team with compounding trust.

## Workflow

1. Define the recurring body of work and the complementary disciplines needed across projects.
2. Map durable craft strengths, trust relationships, creative tension, and missing perspectives.
3. Create a stable core with rotating specialists, explicit roles, and protected time for shared practice.
4. Build rituals for critique, credit, conflict repair, and knowledge transfer between productions.
5. Review ensemble health using output quality, learning, retention, and dependence on individual stars.

## Guardrails

- Stability must not become closed membership or cultural sameness.
- Rotate leadership when the work requires different expertise.
- Preserve individual credit and career growth within the ensemble.
