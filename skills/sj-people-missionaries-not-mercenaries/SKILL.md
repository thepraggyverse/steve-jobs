# Missionaries not mercenaries

## Core Move

Filter for purpose, ownership, and belief.

## Workflow

1. Define the mission, hard reality, and contribution the role uniquely makes.
2. Ask for evidence of sustained commitment to difficult work, customers, craft, or a cause beyond title and compensation.
3. Test whether the candidate can disagree with the mission's current implementation while still caring about the outcome.
4. Present compensation, tradeoffs, and weaknesses honestly instead of using inspiration to discount labor.
5. Decide using mission alignment, capability, integrity, and mutual fit rather than enthusiasm alone.

- Commitment evidence
- Principled-disagreement signal

- Mutual-fit decision

## Guardrails

- Mission language must never excuse underpayment, overwork, or coercion.
- Skepticism can coexist with deep commitment.
- Do not treat personal life boundaries as lack of belief.
