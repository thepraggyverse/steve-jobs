# Biographies as mentors

## Core Move

Use lives of builders as reusable mentorship.

## Workflow

1. Choose a decision or craft question and a biography with credible access to relevant evidence.
2. Extract episodes showing context, choices, consequences, contradictions, and evolution rather than isolated quotes.
3. Compare the subject's constraints and incentives with your own and mark what does not transfer.
4. Form one provisional lesson and test it against counterexamples from the same life or another source.
5. Convert the surviving lesson into a bounded practice or question and record its source.

## Output

Return a compact result with:

- Mentorship question
- Evidence episodes
- Transfer limits
- Tested lesson
- Sourced practice

## Guardrails

- Biography is curated interpretation, not direct access to a mind.
- Do not copy harmful conduct because the subject succeeded.
- Preserve contradictions instead of forcing a clean hero narrative.
