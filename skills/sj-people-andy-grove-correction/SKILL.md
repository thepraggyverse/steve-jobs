# Andy Grove correction

## Core Move

Accept hard correction from a serious operator.

## Workflow

1. Restate the harsh feedback as a specific claim about behavior, decision, or result.
2. Separate delivery style from factual content and identify evidence for and against the claim.
3. Ask what a trusted, informed critic can see that you may be protecting yourself from.
4. Choose what to accept, test, reject, or clarify and tell the giver how you interpreted it.
5. Make one observable correction and set a date to review whether it changed the outcome.

## Output

Return a compact result with:

- Feedback claim
- Evidence assessment
- Defensive blind spot
- Response decision
- Correction and review date

## Guardrails

- Do not accept abuse merely because some criticism is useful.
- Do not dismiss accurate feedback because it arrived badly.
- Correction requires a changed behavior, not a statement of agreement.
