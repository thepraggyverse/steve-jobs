# Design Story

## Core Move

Find the human story that makes the product feel inevitable, then judge every feature, material, and interaction against that story.

## Workflow

1. Name the human situation the artifact enters.
2. Identify the emotional promise, not just the functional job.
3. List every feature, surface, word, or gesture that supports the promise.
4. List every part that feels detached from the story.
5. Rewrite the product direction as one design story and one next artifact to inspect.

## Output

Return a compact result with:

- Human situation
- Design story
- Supporting choices
- Detached choices
- Next artifact

## Guardrails

- Do not turn story into decoration or marketing fluff.
- Keep the story specific enough to cut features.
- If the artifact is absent, state the assumptions you are making.
- Do not use long quotations from the source material.

## Example Prompt

Use $sj-ive-design-story to find the design story for this product before we debate features.
