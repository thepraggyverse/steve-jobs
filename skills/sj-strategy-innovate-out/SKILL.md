# Innovate out

## Core Move

Respond to commoditization with invention.

## Workflow

1. Define the trap in terms of shrinking economics, commoditization, regulation, dependency, or customer behavior.
2. Identify capabilities, assets, and customer trust that remain valuable outside the trapped offering.
3. Generate escape paths that change the basis of competition rather than improving the losing game incrementally.
4. Run the smallest test of customer pull and capability fit while containing downside to the core business.
5. Set evidence thresholds for doubling down, adapting, or exiting before sunk cost takes over.

## Guardrails

- Innovation cannot be a synonym for an unbounded rescue project.
- Protect customers and obligations during transition.
- Do not abandon a viable core before the escape path earns evidence.
