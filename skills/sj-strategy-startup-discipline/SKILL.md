# Startup discipline

## Core Move

Keep scarcity, focus, and truth inside the startup.

## Workflow

1. Establish cash, runway, revenue quality, fixed commitments, hiring load, and the one milestone that changes survival odds.
2. Map every major expense and project to that milestone or a non-negotiable operating duty.
3. Cut vanity, premature scale, and diffuse bets while protecting product quality and critical learning.
4. Set weekly leading indicators, spending authority, and downside triggers before optimism obscures reality.
5. Reforecast from actuals and make the next resource decision while there is still choice.

## Guardrails

- Frugality must not externalize harm, evade obligations, or destroy core quality.
- Revenue volume without margin or collectability is not safety.
- Do not delay bad-news reporting to preserve morale.
