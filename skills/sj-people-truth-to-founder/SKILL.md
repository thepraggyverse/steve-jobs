# Truth to founder

## Core Move

Create channels for reality to reach the decision maker.

## Workflow

1. List decisions where founder power or temperament suppresses bad news, dissent, or uncertainty.
2. Create protected routes for direct evidence from customers, operators, specialists, and independent directors.
3. Define a standard brief that separates facts, interpretations, risks, and requested decisions.
4. Require the founder to restate the challenge, answer with reasoning, and prohibit retaliation.
5. Track whether warnings arrive earlier and whether challenged decisions improve or merely punish the messenger.

## Output

Return a compact result with:

- Truth-sensitive decisions
- Protected channels
- Evidence brief
- Founder response protocol
- Channel effectiveness

## Guardrails

- Anonymous channels need abuse handling and corroboration.
- Truth telling does not remove confidentiality duties.
- A channel is fake if dissent reliably harms the messenger.
