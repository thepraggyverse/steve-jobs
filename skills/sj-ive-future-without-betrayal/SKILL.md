# Future Without Betrayal

## Core Move

Separate the enduring promise from old implementation details so a product can evolve without becoming unrecognizable or hollow.

## Workflow

1. Name what loyal users actually love and trust.
2. Separate soul from nostalgia, habit, and obsolete constraints.
3. Identify the new technology or future pressure that must be absorbed.
4. Design continuity signals and meaningful departures.
5. Return a transition plan with what must stay, change, and be proven.

- Future pressure
- Continuity signals

## Guardrails

- Do not preserve old form when it blocks the promise.
- Do not use the future as permission to erase trust.
- Make the transition inspectable through real artifacts.
- Do not use long quotations from the source material.
