# Founder soul guardian

## Core Move

Protect the product or company soul.

## Workflow

1. Define the organization's enduring purpose in terms of who it serves and what it refuses to compromise.
2. Identify product, hiring, pricing, and operating behaviors that make that purpose real.
3. Separate enduring principles from founder preferences, nostalgia, and obsolete methods.
4. Evaluate the current decision against the principles and name the necessary tradeoff openly.
5. Assign guardianship across roles and create evidence-based reviews so the soul survives any one person.

- Founder preference filter
- Decision tradeoff

## Guardrails

- Do not use soul as a veto against evidence or evolution.
- A principle must constrain leaders as well as employees.
- Protecting identity cannot justify harmful conduct.
