# Manufacturing As Design

## Core Move

Bring implementation, tooling, supply, assembly, and reliability constraints into the design conversation early enough to improve the idea.

## Workflow

1. Identify the production or implementation path the idea requires.
2. List the constraints that could distort the intended experience.
3. Find which constraints are creative assets rather than mere limits.
4. Change the design so the production method strengthens the product story.
5. Name the next build, supplier, or engineering conversation needed.

## Guardrails

- Do not let feasibility flatten ambition too early.
- Do not keep a concept alive if the making process destroys the experience.
- Include maintainability and quality control in the design review.
